# Project brief

- Book: *Diablo: The Kingdom of Shadow*
- Author: Richard A. Knaak
- Source edition: Pocket Books English edition; copyright 2002; ISBN `0-7434-2313-5`
- Genre: Dark fantasy, sword-and-sorcery adventure, supernatural horror, and licensed video-game tie-in fiction
- Primary audience: Mature Traditional Chinese readers who enjoy Diablo lore, dark fantasy, horror, and action-oriented fantasy
- Secondary audience: General mature fantasy readers who may not know the games; retain necessary context from the source without adding lore explanations
- Locale: Taiwan (`zh-Hant-TW`)
- Goal: Faithful, natural, publication-quality translation
- Preserve: Meaning, characterization, tone, imagery, paragraph structure, and intentional ambiguity
- Avoid: Simplified Chinese, abridgment, explanatory additions, and unnatural calques
- Authorial style: Fast-moving commercial fantasy with ominous atmosphere, visceral horror, military dialogue, necromantic imagery, and close character-focused narration
- Localization: Reuse verified series terminology from the completed *Legacy of Blood* project only when the source term occurs here; otherwise establish one consistent Taiwan-style form
- Register: Natural contemporary Taiwan literary prose; retain the dark martial atmosphere without turning narration into faux-classical Chinese or wuxia pastiche
- Content level: Preserve mature violence, demonic imagery, death, bodily horror, and moral ambiguity without softening or embellishing them
- Source condition: UTF-8 ebook text with form-feed pages, fixed-width line wraps, lost emphasis spacing, a duplicated opening preview, and appended author biography/title/TOC; translate only source lines 65–10500
