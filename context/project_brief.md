# Project brief

- Book: *Diablo: Legacy of Blood*
- Author: Richard A. Knaak
- Source edition: Pocket Books English edition; copyright 2001; ISBN `0-7434-2312-7`
- Genre: Dark fantasy, sword-and-sorcery adventure, horror, and licensed video-game tie-in fiction
- Primary audience: Mature Traditional Chinese readers who enjoy Diablo lore, dark fantasy, horror, and action-oriented fantasy
- Secondary audience: General mature fantasy readers who may not know the games; retain necessary context from the source without adding lore explanations
- Locale: Taiwan (`zh-Hant-TW`)
- Goal: Faithful, natural, publication-quality translation
- Preserve: Meaning, characterization, tone, imagery, and intentional ambiguity
- Avoid: Simplified Chinese, unrequested abridgment, explanatory additions, and calques that sound unnatural in Chinese
- Authorial style: Fast-moving commercial fantasy prose with an ominous atmosphere, visceral horror, combat, direct dialogue, and close character-focused narration
- Localization: Prefer established official Taiwan *Diablo* terminology when it can be verified; otherwise transliterate names consistently and record the decision in the glossary
- Register: Natural contemporary Taiwan literary prose; retain the dark, martial atmosphere without turning the narration into faux-classical Chinese or wuxia pastiche
- Content level: Preserve the source's mature violence, demonic imagery, death, and horror without softening or embellishing them
- Source condition: Text-based converted PDF with repeated `ABC Amber LIT Converter` headers and occasional spacing or encoding defects; clean and verify the source text before translation

The translation pilot should test action, dialogue, lore terminology, horror imagery, and character voice before production batches begin.
